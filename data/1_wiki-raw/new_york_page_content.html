This article contains a complete list of Michelin starred restaurants in New York City and Westchester County. The 2006 edition was the first edition of the Michelin Guide to New York City to be published. It was the first time that Michelin published a Red Guide for a region outside Europe.[1] In the 2020 edition, the Guide began to include restaurants outside the cityâ€™s five boroughs, adding Westchester County restaurants to its listing.[2]
